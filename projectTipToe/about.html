About Us

Established in Sydney 


                    
Starting out from a small home garage providing shoes as a white label to various other brands, we have thrived to become a leading shoe manufacturing company in Sydney and Australia. We have grown and expanded our company to provide the highest quality products while never forgetting our roots to help out local businesses and communities.

                        
                    
We are proud to announce that in 2025, we will be launching our own shoe brand at affordable prices. These shoes have been designed for comfort and daily wear.

                
                    
Our Vision is to grow our shoe brand into THE go-to affordable shoe brand in Australia and around the world.
